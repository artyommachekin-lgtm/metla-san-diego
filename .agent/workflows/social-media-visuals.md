# Social Media Visual Strategy — Metla House Cleaning San Diego

## 1. Photo Watermarking Rules
- Every photo posted must have a watermark with: Metla House Cleaning logo + phone (707) 414-8930 + metlahousecleaningsandiego.com
- Placement: Bottom bar (semi-transparent dark strip) OR bottom-right corner overlay
- Keep it readable but not distracting — 15-20% opacity background
- Watermark must be visible even if photo is cropped to square

## 2. Before/After Labeling Standards
- Before photos: Red "BEFORE" label, top-left corner
- After photos: Teal "AFTER" label, top-right corner
- Labels must be bold, all-caps, high contrast
- When posting pairs: before always comes first (left in collages, first in carousels)
- Same angle for both photos whenever possible

## 3. Multi-Photo Post Guidelines by Platform

### Google Business Profile (GBP)
- 1 photo or 1 video per post (GBP limitation)
- Prefer video (30 sec) for "anchor" posts, single photo for standard posts
- Always watermarked
- Before/after collage (side-by-side) works well as single image

### Facebook + Instagram (Carousel — Default Format)
**Carousel is the PRIMARY format for FB+IG posts** (target: ~40% of all Meta posts).

Standard carousel structure:
1. **Image 1 (opener)**: AI-generated cleaner in Metla teal t-shirt — this is the grid/feed thumbnail
2. **Images 2-5**: Real after photos from cleaners' daily OpenPhone submissions
   - Prioritize variety: different rooms (kitchen, bathroom, living room, detail shot)
   - Match the post's neighborhood when possible
   - Screen for privacy (no faces, no personal info, no toilets)

Photo order for carousel: AI opener → best room shot → second room → third room → detail close-up

**Other Meta formats** (rotate with carousels — never same format twice in a row):
- **Reel** (15-30s video): Before/after reveals, process shots. ~30% of posts.
- **Long Video** (30-60s): Full-home walkthroughs. ~20% of posts.
- **Static** (single image): Urgency graphics, testimonials. ~10% of posts.

All photos individually watermarked. "Link in bio" for Instagram.

## 4. Cleaner Photo Guidelines
- Show cleaner(s) from BEHIND — never face the camera
- Branded Metla t-shirt visible (logo on back)
- Working in a real room (cleaning, wiping, scrubbing)
- Can be AI-generated or real photos
- Use sparingly — mix into posts, not every post
- 1 cleaner photo per ~3-4 posts

## 5. Visual Variety Rotation — Two Dimensions

### Dimension 1: Delivery Format (HOW the post is shown)
CRITICAL: Never use the same delivery format twice in a row on the same platform.

| Delivery Format | Target % | Best For |
|-----------------|----------|----------|
| **Carousel** (1 AI opener + 4 real photos) | 40% | Multi-room showcases, general quality. Default for FB+IG. |
| **Reel** (15-30s video) | 30% | Before/after reveals, process shots. |
| **Long Video** (30-60s) | 20% | Full-home walkthroughs. Better for GBP than Meta. |
| **Static** (1 image) | 10% | Urgency graphics (Format E), testimonial cards. |

### Dimension 2: Content Type (WHAT the post shows)
These A-E types can be combined with any delivery format.

| Content Type | When to Use | Photo/Video Needs |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| A. Before/After pair | Dramatic transformations (kitchens, tubs, bathrooms). **Fallback:** If no before photo, reassign to C. | 1 before + 2-3 after shots (wide + detail) |
| B. Cleaner at work | Show the human element, build trust | 1-2 cleaner-from-behind shots + 1-2 room results |
| C. After-only hero shot | Clean, beautiful result | 1 stunning after photo, well-lit, watermarked |
| D. Multi-room showcase | Full-home deep cleans | 4-5 different rooms, all after shots |
| E. Urgency/promo graphic | Seasonal pushes, limited availability | Branded graphic with CTA + phone number |

### How Dimensions Combine
- Carousel + Content D = Multi-room after photos as swipeable album (most common combo)
- Carousel + Content A = Before/after pairs as swipeable slides
- Reel + Content A = Before/after video reveal (dramatic)
- Reel + Content B = Cleaner-at-work process video
- Static + Content E = Urgency graphic image
- Static + Content C = Single hero after shot

## 6. Urgency CTA Bank
Rotate through these — never repeat the same CTA on consecutive posts for the same platform.

1. "Spring spots are filling fast — book your clean this week."
2. "We're booking 2 weeks out in [neighborhood]. Grab your spot."
3. "Limited availability this month in [zip]. Call today."
4. "Our [neighborhood] schedule fills up fast — don't wait."
5. "Only a few openings left this week in [neighborhood]."
6. "[Month] is almost full. Book now for [neighborhood]."
7. "New clients: mention this post for priority scheduling."
8. "We just opened a few slots in [neighborhood]. First come, first served."
9. "Weekend availability going fast in [zip]."
10. "Spring cleaning season is here — [neighborhood] spots are limited."
11. "If you've been putting off a deep clean, now's the time."
12. "Our [neighborhood] team has openings this week. Won't last long."

Rules:
- Replace [neighborhood] with actual neighborhood name
- Replace [zip] with actual ZIP code
- Replace [Month] with current month
- Keep urgency natural, not pushy — we're informing, not pressuring

## 8. Photo Shot List (for every cleaning job)
Capture these on EVERY job:
1. 2-3 "after" shots per room cleaned (wide angle + detail close-up) — EASIEST to get
2. 1 "before" shot per room (before starting, if possible)
3. 1 cleaner-from-behind shot per job (real photo with Metla shirt)
4. Label photos by: location, room type, before/after when saving

Priority: After shots > Before/after pairs > Cleaner shots

## 9. Content Accuracy Reminders
- 1-2 cleaners ONLY in any photos or descriptions (more time, not more people)
- No specialized equipment shown (no HEPA vacuums, steam cleaners unless actually used)
- Cleaning results should be realistic — dramatic but not misleading
- Never show faces of clients or identifiable home exteriors

### Generating More Images
Use Google Gemini (Pro subscription, artyom.machekin@gmail.com) with this prompt template:

> Generate a photorealistic image of a professional house cleaner seen from behind, wearing a teal t-shirt with "Metla House Cleaning" text printed on the back. The cleaner is [ACTION] in a [ROOM DESCRIPTION]. Shot from behind at waist level. Square 1:1 aspect ratio. The teal shirt color should be #14b8a6. No mirrors or reflective surfaces.

**Tips for good results:**
- Simple poses work best (walking, standing, single-arm tasks)
- Avoid mirrors/reflective surfaces (causes arm duplication)
- Avoid complex objects like stovetop grates (gets distorted)
- Avoid two-handed tasks (can generate extra hands)
- Best scenes: wiping counters, making beds, vacuuming, carrying supplies
