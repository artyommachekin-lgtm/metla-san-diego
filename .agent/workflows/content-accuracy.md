## ‚ö†Ô∏è LEGAL RISK: No Over-Promising

**NEVER make guarantees or promises about outcomes the company cannot control.**

### ‚ùå FORBIDDEN Language:
- "Get your deposit back"
- "Ensure you get your deposit"
- "Guarantee your deposit return"
- "We promise X results"
- "100% guaranteed"
- "Will eliminate [problem]"
- Any language that implies a specific outcome

### ‚úÖ SAFE Language:
- "**Maximize your chances** of getting your deposit back"
- "**Help improve** your chances of deposit return"
- "**Designed to meet** property management standards"
- "**Can significantly improve** your chances"
- "Our cleaning **helps satisfy** lease requirements"

### Why This Matters:
Deposits are determined by landlords, not cleaning companies. Promising outcomes we can't control creates **legal liability**. If a customer doesn't get their deposit back after we "guaranteed" it, they could sue.
